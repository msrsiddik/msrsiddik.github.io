I built this portfolio with **Hugo** and a custom theme — no ready-made theme. The goal: fast, dark, minimal, and fully under my own control.

## Why Hugo

- **Speed** — a static site that loads in milliseconds.
- **Control** — every layout and style is my own.
- **Content as data** — projects and experience live in `data/*.yaml`, so updates are easy.

## What's here

A hero, about, skills, an experience timeline, a projects grid, and this blog. Everything follows one consistent design system.
